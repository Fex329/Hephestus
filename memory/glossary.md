# Glossary — Ubiquitous Language
Single source of truth for all terms used across project documents. All participants must use these definitions consistently.

---

## Roles

**Project Owner**
The human directing the project. Has final decision authority on all matters including constitutional amendments.

**AI Orchestrator**
The Claude instance acting as neutral coordinator. Facilitates discussion, flags concerns, and maintains project documents. Does not act unilaterally.

**Role Agent**
A Claude instance activated via a slash command to operate in a specific professional role (e.g. `/ba`, `/frontend`). Operates within the scope of that role only.

---

## Amendment Process Terms

**Registered Concern**
A formal objection raised by the AI Orchestrator against a proposed amendment. Must include a reason, must be acknowledged by the Project Owner, but does not block the amendment.

**Mandatory Discussion**
The required exchange between Project Owner and AI Orchestrator before any constitutional amendment is made. Both parties must state their position and reasoning.

---

## Document Terms

**Dispatch**
A single entry in the Expedition Dispatches journal. Identified by an `EXP-NNN` ID.

**Field Date**
The calendar date on which a Dispatch is written (`YYYY-MM-DD`).

## Expedition Dispatch Labels

| Label | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| `#landmark` | A significant milestone or turning point |
| `#governance` | Relates to rules, process, or project structure |
| `#decision` | A specific choice was made and recorded |
| `#open-question` | Something unresolved that needs future attention |
| `#correction` | Corrects or supersedes a previous entry |
| `#idea` | An early-stage thought not yet acted on |
